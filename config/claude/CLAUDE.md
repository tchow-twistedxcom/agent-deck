## Writing style

NEVER use em dashes (—) or en dashes (–) as sentence connectors in any text: chat replies, Jira/Slack/email drafts, commit messages, PR descriptions, code comments, or documentation. They are a tell of AI-generated text. Use commas, parentheses, periods, semicolons, or colons instead. Hyphenated ranges (0-150 lb) are fine.
